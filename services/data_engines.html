Data Engines

Data engines are designed to efficiently crawl, process, classify and analyze large volumes of data from websites, using advanced NLP techniques to identify text similarity and make relevant recommendations.

Web Crawling

Web crawling is the process of automatically visiting and collecting data from web pages. The data is then stored in a database or used in some other way, such as for analysis or to create a searchable index. However, it's important to note that web crawling can be a complex process and also important to follow the rules and regulations about web scraping, to not cause harm to the source website.

Our engines have been designed to crawl data in a legal and ethical manner, ensuring that the data source is not harmed in any way. It also makes sure that the crawling schedule is friendly and does not cause any inconvenience to the data source.

Data Processing

Our company provides data processing services using advanced NLP techniques. We offer Sentiment Analysis, Text classification, Text Similarity, Text summarization and Text generation. These services provide valuable insights to businesses and organizations. Our team of experts ensure high accuracy and timely delivery of actionable insights to drive business growth.
